**1. How Order Receiving Works (Telegram)**
All customer orders are sent to the Telegram working group: **Na-Ta-Ya Orders**.

*   **Notification:** A message arrives with a hashtag (e.g., `#ORD-123456`).
*   **Order Content:**
    *   **Table:** Table number (if specified).
    *   **Order Details:** List of dishes, quantity, and notes (e.g., "No spicy").
    *   **Total:** Total amount in THB.
*   **Staff Actions:**
    *   The waiter checks the order and sends it to the kitchen.
    *   The bot message has buttons (for internal use):
        *   `üë®‚Äçüç≥ In Progress` ‚Äî Order accepted and cooking.
        *   `‚úÖ Ready/Done` ‚Äî Order is ready and served.

**2. QR Codes and Menu Modes**
The website works in two modes. It is important to understand the difference to help customers.

*   **A. "View Only" Mode**
    *   **For whom:** Passersby, social media, or general browsing.
    *   **Appearance:** Top of the screen says "View Only". There are no "Add to Cart" buttons.
    *   **Link:** The clean website address (e.g., `https://na-ta-ya-menuonline.netlify.app/`).

*   **B. "Ordering Mode"**
    *   **For whom:** Guests who are already seated at a table.
    *   **Appearance:** Top of the screen says "Table 1" (or other number). Guests can add dishes to the cart.
    *   **How to access:** The customer must scan the QR code specific to their table.
    *   **Link format:** The link must end with `?table=NUMBER`.
        *   Table 1: `.../?table=1`
        *   Table 5: `.../?table=5`
    *   **Important:** If a guest complains they cannot order, they likely used the "General" link. Ask them to scan the QR code on *their* table.

    *   **Example Link for Table 1:** `https://na-ta-ya-menuonline.netlify.app/?table=1`
    *   **Create QR Codes here:** `https://qrcod.ru/link/`

**3. Admin Panel (Menu Management)**
Used for the stop-list (hiding dishes), changing prices, or adding new items.

*   **Login:**
    1.  Click the **Lock Icon üîí** at the very bottom of the page (in the footer).
    2.  Enter Administrator credentials:
        *   **Login:** `natayaadmin`
        *   **Password:** `!nataya2025menuchange`

*   **Features:**
    *   **Stop-List (Hide Dish):** Click the **Eye Icon üëÅÔ∏è** next to a dish. The dish will turn gray and disappear from the customer menu. Click again to unhide.
    *   **Editing:** Click the **Pencil ‚úèÔ∏è**. You can change the photo, price, description in 4 languages, or mark as "Spicy".
    *   **Categories:** Select which sections the dish appears in (a dish can be in both "Seafood" and "Tom Yum").
    *   **Add New Dish:** Click the `+ Add Dish` button at the top. Fill in fields and click `Save Item`.
    *   **Manage Categories:** Switch to the **Manage Categories** tab to add new sections (ensure names are provided in all 4 languages).

**4. Technical Notes (Important!)**
*   **Internet:** Customers need internet access to send orders.
*   **Synchronization:**
    *   Since the menu works without a complex server, if you change a price in the Admin Panel on *your* phone, it is saved on *your* device.
    *   *To update the menu globally for all customers permanently, please inform the developer to update the code.*
*   **Errors:** If an order won't send (loading spinner gets stuck), ask the customer to connect to the restaurant Wi-Fi or take the order verbally.
